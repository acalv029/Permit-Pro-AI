⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Coral Springs

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Coral Springs. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ Electronic submittals reviewed in 7 business days vs 15 for hard copy — choose wisely because ONCE YOU CHOOSE, ALL subsequent submittals MUST remain in the same format (electronic OR paper) for the life of that permit
  • ⚠️ Electronic corrections MUST re-submit COMPLETE set of plans with corrections CLEARLY MARKED — partial resubmissions rejected
  • ⚠️ Paper corrections must indicate which disciplines need review by checking appropriate boxes — not checking all required boxes DELAYS review
  • ⚠️ NEW Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application revised 11/14/2025 — NO previous version accepted after December 1, 2025
  • ⚠️ Phone scheduling NO LONGER AVAILABLE as of February 2025 — must use eTrakit ONLY to schedule inspections
  • ⚠️ Cancel inspection before 8:00 AM day of or face reinspection fee ($85.11 Building, $235.72 Fire)
  • ⚠️ Fire Dept reinspection fee ($235.72) is nearly 3x the Building reinspection fee ($85.11) — don't fail fire inspections
  • ⚠️ Truss drawings must be received by Building Department BEFORE foundation inspection — missing = inspection not approved
