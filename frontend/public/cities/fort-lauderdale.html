⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Fort Lauderdale

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Fort Lauderdale. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ Paper applications are NO LONGER ACCEPTED — 100% digital via LauderBuild only. Previously submitted paper apps CANNOT be digitized.
  • ⚠️ LP/Contractor updates and renewals no longer accepted through email or physical mail — must use LauderBuild Contractor Registration Renewal application.
  • ⚠️ Walk-through permit types are ONLY offered through the online portal — no in-person walk-throughs.
  • ⚠️ Forms must be completed in BLACK INK — only signatures may be in standard BLUE INK.
  • ⚠️ Insurance certificate holder MUST read EXACTLY: 'City of Fort Lauderdale, 700 NW 19th Avenue, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33311' — wrong name, wrong address, or abbreviations = REJECTION.
  • ⚠️ When uploading insurance documents to LauderBuild, each document must be uploaded as a SEPARATE PDF to the correct category — do NOT combine multiple documents into one file.
  • ⚠️ NOC is required for jobs over $5,000 (per FAQ). Recorded NOC must be posted at job site prior to FIRST INSPECTION — FL Statutes do NOT require it at application time.
  • ⚠️ The GFL Alliance page says $2,500 NOC threshold but the city FAQ says $5,000. Use $5,000 as the official city position per FAQ.
