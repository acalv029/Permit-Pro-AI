⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Delray Beach

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Delray Beach. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ ALL permits now DIGITAL ONLY through eServices/ePlans — paper plans NOT reviewed since Oct 1, 2024
  • ⚠️ Paper submissions incur $25 scanning fee
  • ⚠️ DOCUMENTS WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED VIA EMAIL — eServices portal only
  • ⚠️ All documents must be unprotected PDF — system rejects password-protected files
  • ⚠️ Applications must be signed AND notarized (confirmed from ePlans Getting Started page)
  • ⚠️ PCN (Parcel Control Number) required — look up at PBC Property Appraiser if unknown
  • ⚠️ Single-page PDF files only (individual plan sheets)
  • ⚠️ All plan sheets must be digitally signed with certificate from third-party certification authority — verified upon upload
