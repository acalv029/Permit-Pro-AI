Building Permit

48 items
  • Submit via SagesGov portal (sagesgov.com/boyntonbeach-fl) — ALL documents must be UNPROTECTED PDF
  • Digital Universal Permit Application built into SagesGov — no paper form needed
  • Plans digitally signed & sealed by FL registered Architect/Engineer — UNPROTECTED PDF only

Roofing Permit

17 items

  • Digital Universal Permit Application built into SagesGov
  • Re-Roof Affidavit/Application — BOYNTON BEACH SPECIFIC form required for all re-roofs

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Boynton Beach

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Boynton Beach. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ All documents must be UNPROTECTED PDF — SagesGov system will REJECT protected files. Tell your design professional!
  • ⚠️ If incorrect project type selected in SagesGov = application ABANDONED, must reapply, payments NOT transferable and NOT refundable
  • ⚠️ Permit #21-2804 or lower must use Legacy system (Click2Gov); new permits use SagesGov — do NOT mix them up
  • ⚠️ All approved documents must be PRINTED from SagesGov portal and made available to field inspectors on site
  • ⚠️ NOC threshold $5,000 general, $15,000 for HVAC repair/replacement — email recorded & certified NOC to BuildingM@bbfl.us
  • ⚠️ Inspection requests after 3:00 PM are NOT scheduled for the next business day
  • ⚠️ Need permit application number AND 7-digit PIN for scheduling inspections online
  • ⚠️ Wait for ALL plan review disciplines to complete before submitting corrections — submitting before all reviews can trigger resubmittal fee clock
