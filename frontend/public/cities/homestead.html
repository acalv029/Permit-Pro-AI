Common Rejection Reasons

These are the top reasons permit packages get rejected in Homestead. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

1
Insurance certificate holder MUST read EXACTLY: 'CITY OF HOMESTEAD, 100 CIVIC COURT, HOMESTEAD, FL 33030' — this is from the official Contractor Licensing Registration PDF. Private Provider cert holder is slightly different: 'City of Homestead Building Department, 100 Civic Court, Homestead, FL 33030'
2
Contractor must register with BOTH Miami-Dade County AND City of Homestead BEFORE submitting ANY permit application — dual registration is mandatory
3
As of October 1, 2025, ALL contractor/utility owner registration must go through EPL-B.U.I.L.D. — the old paper/email registration process is dead
4
Registration requires: (A) State of Florida Contractor License (DBPR), (B) Miami-Dade County Local Business Tax Receipt OR County of Origin LBT, (C) Liability & Workers Comp Insurance, (D) Driver's License (original or clear color copy), (E) Company info + EIN + email
5
Owner-builder insurance minimums are DIFFERENT from contractor: $50,000 per person liability (per official registration page)
6
File naming convention is STRICTLY ENFORCED — application will NOT be accepted if naming convention is not followed! Format: BD-YY-XXXXX-PT-R-DISCIPLINE (all UPPERCASE). If no permit number yet, use file contents as name
7
NO special characters allowed in file names: no asterisks, parentheses, periods, question marks, shapes, double periods, # % & { } / \ ? < > * $ ! ' — this alone causes more rejections than any other issue
8
Plans must be ONE PDF PER DISCIPLINE — grouped by Architectural, Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Civil. NOT one giant PDF, NOT one PDF per page

Frequently Asked Questions — Homestead Permits

What documents do I need for a building permit in Homestead?

Requirements vary by permit type, but typically include: signed & sealed plans, a completed permit application, Notice of Commencement (if above the NOC threshold), product approvals (NOAs/FL numbers), energy calculations, and contractor licensing documents. Our AI checks your specific package against all 294 requirements for Homestead.

How do I submit a permit application in Homestead?

Electronic only via EPL-B.U.I.L.D. — strict file naming convention REQUIRED You can access the online portal at Homestead Permit Portal.

Does Homestead require a Notice of Commencement (NOC)?

Yes. Homestead requires a Notice of Commencement for permitted work exceeding $2500 in value. The NOC must be recorded with the county clerk and displayed at the job site before the first inspection.

Is Homestead in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ)?

Yes — Homestead is in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone. All exterior products (windows, doors, roofing) must have Miami-Dade NOA (Notice of Acceptance) or Florida Product Approval. Standard building code approvals are NOT sufficient.
