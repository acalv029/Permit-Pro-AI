Building Permit — New Construction / Alterations / Additions

25 items
  • Hialeah Permit Application — signed & notarized by BOTH owner AND qualifier (separate permit for each trade discipline)
  • 2 Sets of Plans — signed & sealed by licensed FL PE or Registered Architect
  • Contractor Registration with City of Hialeah — must be registered PRIOR to submittal with all license/insurance current

Roofing Permit — Re-Roof / New Roof / Repair (HVHZ)

22 items
  • HVHZ Uniform Roofing Permit Application (2 copies) — FBC 8th Edition (2023); Sections A-E as applicable per roof type
  • Hialeah Permit Application — signed & notarized by owner AND qualifier
  • Roofing Permit Fee Sheet (Rev March 20, 2026) — minimum roofing permit fee is $290.00

Plumbing Permit

14 items
  • Hialeah Permit Application (Plumbing checked) — signed & notarized
  • Plumbing Fee Sheet (Rev March 20, 2026)
  • Contractor Registration + Insurance — cert holder verbatim: 'City of Hialeah as certificate holder'

Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU)

14 items
  • Hialeah Permit Application — signed & notarized (building permit type)
  • Product Approvals / NOAs (2 copies) — Miami-Dade NOA for all exterior components (HVHZ — 175 mph)
  • Energy Calculations — ACCA Manual J, S, D and EPL display card when HVAC installed

Electrical Permit — Service Change / Wiring / Shutters / Windows & Doors

13 items
  • Electrical Fee Sheet (Rev March 20, 2026)

Window & Door Replacement Permit

Fence Permit — Wood, Chain Link, Iron, Concrete, PVC, Rolling Gates, Bollards

Driveway Permit

9 items

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Hialeah

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Hialeah. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ ALL applications MUST be NOTARIZED — strictly enforced, automatic rejection without it
  • ⚠️ Owner affidavits MUST also be NOTARIZED — both owner AND qualifier signatures
  • ⚠️ For condos: Association Authorization Letter with president's signature NOTARIZED required for ALL permits
  • ⚠️ Owner-builder: Must reside at property with valid FL DL matching address; warranty deed + homestead exemption may be required; corporate-owned properties CANNOT use owner-builder exemption
  • ⚠️ Tenant improvements limited to 500 sq ft OR under $5,000 for non-structural only — otherwise licensed contractor required
  • ⚠️ DERM approval required BEFORE city permit for commercial projects, apartment buildings, and townhouse projects
  • ⚠️ NOC threshold: $2,500 per FL Statute 713.135(1)(d) and city General Permits Requirements PDF; $15,000 exemption for HVAC per FL Statute 713.02(5)
  • ⚠️ NOC recorded at Miami-Dade County Recorder, 22 NW 1st St, Miami, FL 33128; hours 9 AM-4 PM; fees $10 first page + $8.50 subsequent; phone (305) 275-1155 press 1-6-1-0
