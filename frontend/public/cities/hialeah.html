Common Rejection Reasons

8 documented for Hialeah. Our AI flags all of these.

1ALL applications MUST be NOTARIZED — strictly enforced, automatic rejection without it
2Owner affidavits MUST also be NOTARIZED — both owner AND qualifier signatures
3For condos: Association Authorization Letter with president's signature NOTARIZED required for ALL permits
4Owner-builder: Must reside at property with valid FL DL matching address; warranty deed + homestead exemption may be required; corporate-owned properties CANNOT use owner-builder exemption
5Tenant improvements limited to 500 sq ft OR under $5,000 for non-structural only — otherwise licensed contractor required
6DERM approval required BEFORE city permit for commercial projects, apartment buildings, and townhouse projects
7NOC threshold: $2,500 per FL Statute 713.135(1)(d) and city General Permits Requirements PDF; $15,000 exemption for HVAC per FL Statute 713.02(5)
8NOC recorded at Miami-Dade County Recorder, 22 NW 1st St, Miami, FL 33128; hours 9 AM-4 PM; fees $10 first page + $8.50 subsequent; phone (305) 275-1155 press 1-6-1-0
