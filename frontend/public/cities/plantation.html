⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Plantation

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Plantation. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ Application must be signed and notarized by QUALIFIER
  • ⚠️ Digital ONLY since 11/14/2022 via Accela Citizen Access (ACA) — NO paper applications accepted
  • ⚠️ File naming convention MANDATORY: use 2-letter prefix (BD-Roof, EL-Generator, PL-Irrigation, ME-HVAC, etc.) followed by hyphen and description — incorrectly named files REJECTED before review
  • ⚠️ Digital signatures must be certificate-authority backed — regular electronic signatures REJECTED; if not digitally signed, submit notarized affidavit from designer of record
  • ⚠️ Reserve 4x4 inch space in top right corner of ALL plan sheets for City's digital approval stamp
  • ⚠️ Scanned drawings must be 300 DPI minimum
  • ⚠️ When submitting corrections: do NOT resubmit entire plan set — only upload changed pages with same sheet numbers for proper versioning
  • ⚠️ Mid-cycle uploads NOT permitted — must wait until plan review cycle is complete (Hold status) before uploading corrections
