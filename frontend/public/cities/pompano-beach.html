Common Rejection Reasons

Work without permit = DOUBLE the standard permit fee

FAQ — Pompano Beach

What documents do I need for a permit in Pompano Beach?

For most permits: completed application, signed & sealed plans, site survey, product approvals (Miami-Dade NOA or FL# required since Pompano Beach is HVHZ), energy calculations for HVAC, and Notice of Commencement if your job exceeds $2500. Is Pompano Beach in the HVHZ?

Yes — Pompano Beach is in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone. Every exterior product (windows, doors, roofing, shutters) must have a Miami-Dade NOA or Florida Product Approval with FL# number. Standard ICC-ES reports or generic spec sheets are NOT accepted. Our AI verifies product approval documentation automatically.
