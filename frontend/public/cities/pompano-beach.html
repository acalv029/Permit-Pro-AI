FAQ — Pompano Beach Permits

What documents do I need for a building permit in Pompano Beach?

For most Pompano Beach permits you'll need: a completed permit application, signed & sealed plans by a licensed FL architect or engineer, a site survey, product approvals (NOAs for HVHZ or FL# numbers), energy calculations (Manual J/D for HVAC), and a Notice of Commencement if the job exceeds $2500. How do I submit a permit in Pompano Beach?

100% Electronic via ePlan - Applications must be in BLACK INK You can access the portal at Pompano Beach's online permit system. Call 954-786-4669 for questions.

Is Pompano Beach in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone?

Yes — Pompano Beach is in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ). This means ALL exterior products — windows, doors, roofing, shutters — must have either a Miami-Dade NOA (Notice of Acceptance) or Florida Product Approval with a valid FL# number. Generic product spec sheets or ICC-ES reports are NOT accepted as substitutes. Our AI automatically flags products that lack proper HVHZ approvals.
