⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Pompano Beach

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Pompano Beach. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ Applications MUST be in BLACK INK - will be rejected otherwise
  • ⚠️ Fire Review Application required for multi-family/condo/commercial — NOT required for single-family residential
  • ⚠️ Broward County EPD must be approved BEFORE city submittal — submitting without EPD = rejection
  • ⚠️ Both owner AND contractor signatures required, notarized — missing either = rejection
  • ⚠️ Different NOC thresholds: General >$2,500, Most trades >$5,000, Roofing >$7,500
  • ⚠️ New/relocated electrical service must be UNDERGROUND per City Ordinance 152.07
  • ⚠️ Emergency A/C repairs: notify Chief Mechanical Inspector (Scott.Maxwell@copbfl.com) with job address BEFORE starting — submit permit next business day
  • ⚠️ Work without permit = DOUBLE the standard permit fee
