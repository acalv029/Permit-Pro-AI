FAQ — Miami

What documents do I need for a permit in Miami?

For most permits: completed application, signed & sealed plans, site survey, product approvals (Miami-Dade NOA or FL# required since Miami is HVHZ), energy calculations for HVAC, and Notice of Commencement if your job exceeds $5000. Is Miami in the HVHZ?

Yes — Miami is in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone. Every exterior product (windows, doors, roofing, shutters) must have a Miami-Dade NOA or Florida Product Approval with FL# number. Standard ICC-ES reports or generic spec sheets are NOT accepted. Our AI verifies product approval documentation automatically.
