Common Rejection Reasons

Top 8 reasons permits get rejected in Deerfield Beach.

1
NOC thresholds VARY by permit type — General: $2,500; Fence: $5,000; Windows/Doors: $5,000; A/C Change Out: $15,000; Demolition: $5,000. Using the wrong threshold = rejection.
2
NOC must be RECORDED and submitted AT SUBMITTAL (prior to permit issuance) — not at first inspection like most Broward cities.
3
Insurance certificate holder must read EXACTLY: 'The City of Deerfield Beach / 150 NE 2 Avenue / Deerfield Beach, FL 33441' — pulled from Contractor Registration Form PDF.
4
ALL permit applications require OWNER SIGNATURE — no exceptions. Missing owner signature = automatic rejection.
5
HOA Affidavit is required for ALL residential permits — even if property is NOT in an HOA. If property IS in an HOA, also need formal HOA approval letter for Zoning review.
6
Product Approvals (NOAs) must be stamped 'approved' by the Designer of Record (DOR) — raw/unstamped NOAs are NOT accepted. This is stricter than most Broward cities.
7
Asbestos Statement (SRRA) is MANDATORY for ALL re-roof submittals — no exceptions. Highlighted on city website.
8
Condo owners CANNOT do their own work — must hire a licensed contractor. Failure is a FELONY under F.S. 489.127(1)(f).
