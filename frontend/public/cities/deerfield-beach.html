Building Permit

43 items
  • Submit via CAP Government online portal (since 12/15/2025) or in-person at 150 NE 2 Avenue, Deerfield Beach, FL 33441
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application — BLACK INK, signed by Owner AND Contractor
  • Two (2) complete sets of plans signed and sealed by Designer of Record (as required by code)

Roofing Permit

21 items
  • Submit via CAP Government online portal (since 12/15/2025) or in-person
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application — BLACK INK, OWNER SIGNATURE REQUIRED
  • DFB-specific Roofing Application & Forms — separate form, also requires owner signature

Residential Generator Permit

21 items
  • Submit via CAP Government online portal or in-person
  • Building permit application AND electrical permit required
  • Generator location must conform to local zoning requirements and be above base flood level

Windows and Doors Permit

18 items
  • Submit via CAP Government online portal or in-person at Building Division in City Hall
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (Structural) — completely filled out
  • Broward County Retrofit Window & Door Schedule — completely filled out; 2 sets if in-person

Electrical Permit

16 items
  • Submit via CAP Government online portal (since 12/15/2025) or in-person
  • Broward County Uniform Permit Application (Electrical checked) — BLACK INK
  • Application must be signed by Owner AND trade Contractor

Mechanical / HVAC Permit

16 items
  • Submit via CAP Government online portal (since 12/15/2025) or in-person
  • Broward County Uniform Permit Application (Mechanical checked) — BLACK INK
  • Application must be signed by Owner AND trade Contractor

Demolition Permit

16 items
  • Submit via CAP Government online portal or in-person
  • Broward County Uniform Permit Application — signed AND NOTARIZED by owner and contractor for EACH discipline

Fence Permit

15 items
  • Submit via CAP Government online portal or in-person
  • Copy of signed and sealed property survey showing location, height, and type of fence and gates

Dock / Seawall / Marine Structure Permit

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Deerfield Beach

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Deerfield Beach. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ NOC thresholds VARY by permit type — General: $2,500; Fence: $5,000; Windows/Doors: $5,000; A/C Change Out: $15,000; Demolition: $5,000. Using the wrong threshold = rejection.
  • ⚠️ NOC must be RECORDED and submitted AT SUBMITTAL (prior to permit issuance) — not at first inspection like most Broward cities.
  • ⚠️ Insurance certificate holder must read EXACTLY: 'The City of Deerfield Beach / 150 NE 2 Avenue / Deerfield Beach, FL 33441' — pulled from Contractor Registration Form PDF.
  • ⚠️ ALL permit applications require OWNER SIGNATURE — no exceptions. Missing owner signature = automatic rejection.
  • ⚠️ HOA Affidavit is required for ALL residential permits — even if property is NOT in an HOA. If property IS in an HOA, also need formal HOA approval letter for Zoning review.
  • ⚠️ Product Approvals (NOAs) must be stamped 'approved' by the Designer of Record (DOR) — raw/unstamped NOAs are NOT accepted. This is stricter than most Broward cities.
  • ⚠️ Asbestos Statement (SRRA) is MANDATORY for ALL re-roof submittals — no exceptions. Highlighted on city website.
  • ⚠️ Condo owners CANNOT do their own work — must hire a licensed contractor. Failure is a FELONY under F.S. 489.127(1)(f).
