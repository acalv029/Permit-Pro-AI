Common Rejection Reasons

8 documented for Deerfield Beach. Our AI flags all of these.

1NOC thresholds VARY by permit type — General: $2,500; Fence: $5,000; Windows/Doors: $5,000; A/C Change Out: $15,000; Demolition: $5,000. Using the wrong threshold = rejection.
2NOC must be RECORDED and submitted AT SUBMITTAL (prior to permit issuance) — not at first inspection like most Broward cities.
3Insurance certificate holder must read EXACTLY: 'The City of Deerfield Beach / 150 NE 2 Avenue / Deerfield Beach, FL 33441' — pulled from Contractor Registration Form PDF.
4ALL permit applications require OWNER SIGNATURE — no exceptions. Missing owner signature = automatic rejection.
5HOA Affidavit is required for ALL residential permits — even if property is NOT in an HOA. If property IS in an HOA, also need formal HOA approval letter for Zoning review.
6Product Approvals (NOAs) must be stamped 'approved' by the Designer of Record (DOR) — raw/unstamped NOAs are NOT accepted. This is stricter than most Broward cities.
7Asbestos Statement (SRRA) is MANDATORY for ALL re-roof submittals — no exceptions. Highlighted on city website.
8Condo owners CANNOT do their own work — must hire a licensed contractor. Failure is a FELONY under F.S. 489.127(1)(f).
