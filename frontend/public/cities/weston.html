Common Rejection Reasons

8 documented for City of Weston. Our AI flags all of these.

1NOC threshold for A/C Change-Out is $15,000 — NOT $2,500 and NOT $7,500. Per Quick Permit Application Checklist (Rev 3/2025). Standard NOC threshold for all other work is $2,500.
2Insurance cert holder wording is just 'City of Weston' — no address required on certificate. Per Contractor Registration Form Rev 7/2025.
3License and insurance required for EVERY submittal (not just first registration) — only exception is revisions. More frequent requirement than most cities.
4Separate check required for EACH trade application — cannot combine payment for multiple trades on one check.
5CASH NOT ACCEPTED for any permit fees — checks and credit cards only.
6Copy of original signature is NOT acceptable on any document — must be original or notarized. This applies to BCUBPA, Permit Acknowledgement Affidavit, and all owner signatures.
7Document/file naming convention is MANDATORY — incorrect file names result in DENIAL of review and DELAY of permit. Use Weston Electronic File Naming Conventions document.
8Plans must be submitted as SINGLE-PAGE files — do not combine. Name each file by sheet number (A.1, M.1, E.4, S.2).
