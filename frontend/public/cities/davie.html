Common Rejection Reasons

8 documented for Town of Davie. Our AI flags all of these.

1CLOSED FRIDAYS — Town of Davie operates Monday-Thursday only (7:30 AM - 5:30 PM); do not attempt to schedule inspections or visit on Fridays
2NOC must be POSTED at job site for first inspection (7 days after permit issued) — if not posted, inspection fails and $75 re-inspection fee is charged
3Inspection cancellation cutoff is 8:30 AM — cancellations after 8:30 AM may result in additional fees on the permit
4OAS (Online Application Submittal) is the REQUIRED method for all permit submissions as of January 12, 2026 — paper submissions are limited to walk-through only
5Jobs valued at $100,000+ require the application/drop-off fee to be paid BEFORE the permit is routed to plan review
6Walk-through permits (Wednesdays 8-10:30 AM) are one-time review — if rejected, the entire package is returned and must be resubmitted as regular review through OAS
7Broward County updated the standard Building Permit Form — as of 03/09/2026 the new form is REQUIRED; old forms will be rejected
8Survey Affidavit is required if survey is older than 2 years AND there have been no changes to the property — without the affidavit a new survey is needed
