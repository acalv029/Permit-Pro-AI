Frequently Asked Questions — Town of Davie Permits

What documents do I need for a building permit in Town of Davie?

Requirements vary by permit type, but typically include: signed & sealed plans, a completed permit application, Notice of Commencement (if above the NOC threshold), product approvals (NOAs/FL numbers), energy calculations, and contractor licensing documents. How do I submit a permit application in Town of Davie?

Contact the Town of Davie Building Department for current submission procedures.

Does Town of Davie require a Notice of Commencement (NOC)?

Yes. Town of Davie requires a Notice of Commencement for permitted work exceeding $$2,500 in value. The NOC must be recorded with the county clerk and displayed at the job site before the first inspection.

Is Town of Davie in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ)?

Yes — Town of Davie is in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone. All exterior products (windows, doors, roofing) must have Miami-Dade NOA (Notice of Acceptance) or Florida Product Approval. Standard building code approvals are NOT sufficient.
