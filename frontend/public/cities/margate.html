⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Margate

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Margate. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ Broward County Uniform Application MUST be the January 8, 2026 revised version — effective March 9, 2026 NO previous versions accepted. Address on page 2 is MANDATORY.
  • ⚠️ Insurance certificate holder MUST read EXACTLY: 'City of Margate, 901 NW 66th Avenue, Ste B, Margate, Fl 33063' — note 'Ste B' and 'Fl' (not 'FL'). Pulled from Contractor Registration PDF.
  • ⚠️ ALL applications must be in BLACK INK — blue ink will be rejected.
  • ⚠️ Margate requires THREE (3) sets of energy calculations — more than any other Broward city.
  • ⚠️ Proof of ownership AND proof of cost (signed contract) required with EVERY permit — unique Margate requirement beyond standard Broward.
  • ⚠️ NOC threshold for roofing is $5,000 (not $2,500 like most Margate permits). Per roofing checklist PDF.
  • ⚠️ NOC threshold for AC changeouts is $7,500 (higher than standard $2,500). Per Alliance info.
  • ⚠️ NOC threshold for docks is $5,000 (not $2,500). Per dock checklist PDF.
