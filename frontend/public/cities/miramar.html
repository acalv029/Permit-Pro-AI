📋 Permit Types We Check

Building Permit - Miramar

95 items
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (BLACK INK; signed & notarized by Owner AND Qualifier)
  • Four (4) sets of plans signed & sealed by Professional Engineer or Registered Architect
  • Plans must indicate: Occupancy type/load, Construction Type, SF, all details per scope

Windows & Doors Permit - Miramar

19 items
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (BLACK INK; signed by Owner & Contractor)
  • One (1) set Floor-Plan Sketch indicating location & dimension for EVERY proposed door & window
  • Openings ID on sketch must match the Broward County Uniform Windows & Doors schedule

Plumbing Permit - Miramar

15 items
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (Plumbing checked; BLACK INK; signed by Owner & Contractor)
  • Plans as required per scope of work
  • Affidavit of Awareness of Homeowner's Association (MANDATORY even if NOT in HOA)

Roofing / Re-Roof Permit - Miramar

15 items
  • Roof Plan (part of the complete HVHZ package)
  • Roof Underlayment Affidavit (MIRAMAR-SPECIFIC — per FBC 2023)

Swimming Pool & Spa Permit - Miramar

15 items
  • All required applications based on scope of work (Structural, Plumbing, Electrical, etc.)
  • Affidavit of Identical Documents (for digitally signed/sealed plans)
  • Full scalable updated Boundary Survey (1 set if plans 11x17; 2 sets if plans larger; within 7 years; original s/s)

Demolition Permit - Miramar

15 items
  • Architectural Plans indicating location, area, and complete scope of proposed demolition
  • Commercial: FOUR (4) sets signed & sealed by Engineer or Registered Architect
  • Residential partial demolition: TWO (2) original sets signed & sealed by Engineer or Architect

Solar System Permit - Miramar

12 items
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (BLACK INK; signed by Owner & Contractor)
  • One (1) set of plans signed & sealed by PE or RA showing location & attachment of equipment
  • Plans should not exceed 11"x17" page size; if larger, TWO (2) sets required

Electrical Permit - Miramar

11 items
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (Electrical checked; BLACK INK; signed by Owner & Contractor)
  • Miramar Electrical Service Change Application Package (MIRAMAR-SPECIFIC — dedicated package for service changes)
  • Four (4) sets of plans signed & sealed (for service change — MIRAMAR requires 4 sets, not 2)

Mechanical / HVAC Permit - Miramar

11 items
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (Mechanical checked; BLACK INK)
  • Permit application describing full scope of work
  • One (1) set AC Replacement Sheet (MIRAMAR-SPECIFIC form)

Driveway / Slab / Paving Permit - Miramar

10 items
  • Slab, Driveway, and Paving Application Package (MIRAMAR-SPECIFIC comprehensive package)
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (BLACK INK)
  • Plans/site plan showing proposed work, dimensions, property lines, setbacks

Fence Permit - Miramar

9 items
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (BLACK INK)
  • Site plan showing fence location, property lines, setbacks
  • Product Approvals / NOAs for fence materials (aluminum, PVC, wood, chain link)

Screen Enclosure / Patio / Aluminum Roof Permit - Miramar

9 items
  • Screen Enclosure/Patio/Aluminum Roof Application Package (MIRAMAR-SPECIFIC combined package)
  • Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (BLACK INK)
  • Plans signed & sealed showing structure, dimensions, attachment details

⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Miramar

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Miramar. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ Building Department CLOSED ON FRIDAYS — Mon-Thur 7 AM - 6 PM only
  • ⚠️ Applications must be in BLACK INK — other colors will be rejected
  • ⚠️ Do NOT highlight any information on plans — highlighted plans will be REJECTED
  • ⚠️ Construction Debris Removal Affidavit MANDATORY for ALL permits (City Code §18-7)
  • ⚠️ HOA Awareness Affidavit required even if property is NOT in an HOA (City Code Sec. 22-29(c)(1))
  • ⚠️ Debris must be removed by Waste Pro of Florida ONLY — failure = code violation with fines
  • ⚠️ NOC threshold: $5,000 general, $15,000 for A/C (much higher than most cities!)
  • ⚠️ NOC must be RECORDED and available at FIRST INSPECTION — not at submittal
