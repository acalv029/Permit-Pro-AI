⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Oakland Park

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Oakland Park. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ Insurance certificate holder MUST read EXACTLY: 'City of Oakland Park, 1100 Park Lane East, First Floor, Oakland Park, FL 33334' — confirmed from CURRENT Contractor Registration Requirements PDF AND Pool/Spa Packet (both updated). Older permit packets (dock, seawall, driveway) still show outdated addresses. The current registration PDF is authoritative.
  • ⚠️ ALL applications MUST use the Broward County Uniform Building Permit Application (Rev 11/14/25) — no other form accepted. Old revisions rejected. Download current version from city website or Broward County.
  • ⚠️ ALL applications and documents must be completely filled out, SIGNED, and NOTARIZED — unnotarized applications rejected at intake.
  • ⚠️ HVHZ city — ALL exterior product approvals MUST be Miami-Dade County NOAs (Notice of Acceptance). Florida Product Approvals alone NOT sufficient. Expired NOAs = automatic rejection.
  • ⚠️ NOC threshold is $2,500 for all trades EXCEPT HVAC which is $7,500. NOC must be recorded through Broward County BEFORE submitting to Oakland Park. One recorded copy required before first inspection.
  • ⚠️ Online submittals — ALL documents must be PDF format ONLY (no img, jpeg, tiff). Plans as ONE combined PDF. Application as SEPARATE PDF. Do NOT lock, restrict, or add security to PDFs — prevents staff from opening/stamping.
  • ⚠️ Digital seals required for online submittals — plans must be DIGITALLY signed by design professional. Wet ink on scanned copy NOT accepted for digital submittals.
  • ⚠️ Roofing permits require BOTH the Broward County Uniform Application AND the Roofing HVHZ-Uniform Permit Application (Rev 2023). Submitting only one = incomplete.
