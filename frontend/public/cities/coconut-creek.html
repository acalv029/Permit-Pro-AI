Frequently Asked Questions — Coconut Creek Permits

What documents do I need for a building permit in Coconut Creek?

Requirements vary by permit type, but typically include: signed & sealed plans, a completed permit application, Notice of Commencement (if above the NOC threshold), product approvals (NOAs/FL numbers), energy calculations, and contractor licensing documents. Our AI checks your specific package against all 215 requirements for Coconut Creek.

How do I submit a permit application in Coconut Creek?

Electronic via ePermits; owner-builders must apply IN-PERSON You can access the online portal at Coconut Creek Permit Portal.

Does Coconut Creek require a Notice of Commencement (NOC)?

Yes. Coconut Creek requires a Notice of Commencement for permitted work exceeding $2500 in value. The NOC must be recorded with the county clerk and displayed at the job site before the first inspection.

Is Coconut Creek in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ)?

Yes — Coconut Creek is in a High Velocity Hurricane Zone. All exterior products (windows, doors, roofing) must have Miami-Dade NOA (Notice of Acceptance) or Florida Product Approval. Standard building code approvals are NOT sufficient.
