Common Rejection Reasons

8 documented for Lauderdale-by-the-Sea. Our AI flags all of these.

1Insurance cert holder must read EXACTLY: 'Town of Lauderdale By-The-Sea, 4501 N. Ocean Drive, Lauderdale By-The-Sea, FL 33308' — note the hyphens and full address are REQUIRED per Contractor Registration page
2NOC must be RECORDED at Broward County BEFORE building dept submittal — this is the #1 rejection reason in LBTS
3EPD approval required PRIOR to building dept submittal for: new construction, additions, alterations to non-residential, demolitions, generators, commercial/multi-family pools, underground storage tanks — submit ONLINE for security code & application number, walk-thru review
4Trade applications must be in BLACK INK — will be rejected otherwise
5Contract must show itemized price breakdown for ALL trades — not just a lump sum
6TURTLE LIGHTING REQUIREMENT (LBTS-specific): Any window or door visible from the beach must have tinted glass with VLT ≤ 0.45 — applies to seaward and shore-perpendicular sides of structure. Must submit documented proof of VLT compliance
7Time limits for construction are enforced — new construction/additions must be complete within 18 months, exterior repair/renovation within 6 months. Failure results in fines or legal action by the Town
8Demolition permits expire in 60 DAYS (not 180) — much shorter than standard permits
