⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons — Lauderdale-by-the-Sea

These are the most common reasons permit packages get rejected in Lauderdale-by-the-Sea. Our AI flags all of these automatically.

  • ⚠️ Insurance cert holder must read EXACTLY: 'Town of Lauderdale By-The-Sea, 4501 N. Ocean Drive, Lauderdale By-The-Sea, FL 33308' — note the hyphens and full address are REQUIRED per Contractor Registration page
  • ⚠️ NOC must be RECORDED at Broward County BEFORE building dept submittal — this is the #1 rejection reason in LBTS
  • ⚠️ EPD approval required PRIOR to building dept submittal for: new construction, additions, alterations to non-residential, demolitions, generators, commercial/multi-family pools, underground storage tanks — submit ONLINE for security code & application number, walk-thru review
  • ⚠️ Trade applications must be in BLACK INK — will be rejected otherwise
  • ⚠️ Contract must show itemized price breakdown for ALL trades — not just a lump sum
  • ⚠️ TURTLE LIGHTING REQUIREMENT (LBTS-specific): Any window or door visible from the beach must have tinted glass with VLT ≤ 0.45 — applies to seaward and shore-perpendicular sides of structure. Must submit documented proof of VLT compliance
  • ⚠️ Time limits for construction are enforced — new construction/additions must be complete within 18 months, exterior repair/renovation within 6 months. Failure results in fines or legal action by the Town
  • ⚠️ Demolition permits expire in 60 DAYS (not 180) — much shorter than standard permits
